## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Where do I see the new features?**
A: Just use the site normally! Scroll to see animations, fill the form to see validation.

**Q: How do I customize animations?**
A: Edit `assets/css/enhancements.css` and change animation timing (0.6s default).

**Q: Can I turn off animations?**
A: Yes, see [ENHANCEMENTS.md](ENHANCEMENTS.md) under "Accessibility" for reduced-motion support.

**Q: My form isn't sending emails?**
A: Update EmailJS credentials in `assets/js/enhancements.js` line 97.

**Q: Works on mobile?**
A: Yes! All features are fully responsive and mobile-optimized.
